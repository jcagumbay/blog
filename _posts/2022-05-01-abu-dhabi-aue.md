We stayed in Abu Dhabi the whole time during our vacation. Aside from the Expo and the Liwa camping, we managed to visit some sites in Abu Dhabi. We went to the Louvre museum, fun ride at Warner Bros, Emirates Park Zoo, Abu Dhabi Grand Masjid but most of the time, we spent it going back and forth to get tested for Covid :P

There are more things to do in the city specially if you like heart pumping rides. The extreme rides, specifically at the Ferrari World, are not for everyone and I'm pretty sure it's not for me. But it's a must try for every thrill seekers out there. If you have a chance to try it, please do try it. However, you might want to wait a little longer for the pandemic to end.
