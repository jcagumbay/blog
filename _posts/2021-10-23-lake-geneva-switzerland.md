Our annual company team gathering was postponed this year. The winter Verbier escapade was moved to summer in Lausanne.
We were there for a couple of days together with Iris. One day before we went back to France, we took the cruise on Lake Geneva. I've been to Lausanne for a couple of times but I've never tried the cruise. Probably because I usually go to Lausanne in winter. And, well, aside from fact that, I don't like to be on it alone. :P
There are many routes you can choose from but we took the one that starts from Lausanne to Chillon castle with three stops at St. Gingolph, Port-Valais and Villeneuve in between. It was a great experience and is highly recommended, on top of taking a dip on the lake, when you are about to visit Lausanne.
