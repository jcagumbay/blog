We went down south for a weekend on Hannah's birthday. Perpignan, a small French city near the Mediterranean coast and the border with Spain, is more or less 6 hours train ride from Paris.

There was really not much to do around the city. We went for the usual tourist routes and explore a little bit outside the city. There was a food event of some sort called Pyrénéon where we had our brunch. We went to the coast and stayed there for a couple of hours and had some drinks. Of course, the sea was a refreshing sight.

Unfortunately, Iris and I had to leave early because we need to catch the last train going home. One of the inconveniences of living in the forest. :P
