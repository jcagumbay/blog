Once again, we went to Strasbourg. The first time we were here was on Christmas day in 2018. The city is known as Christmas Capital and anyone who has a chance to visit here during this season must not have second thoughts. It's indeed truly amazing. I'm sure that our stay there would would have been more magical if not due to the mass shooting prior to our arrival.

We chose to go back to the city since it has good connections to neighboring cities, specifically Colmar and Basel. We didn't spend much time here since we already walked mostly around the city before. Probably one of the highlights was whiskey tasting even though I had no idea what I was doing. Eventually, I got convinced to buy one. :P
