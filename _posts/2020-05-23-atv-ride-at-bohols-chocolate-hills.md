I've been to these famous hills even before it gained popularity among local and international tourists. In fact, my grandparent's house was literally on top of a hill. Chocolate hills are scattered throughout three towns but the famous attraction is in Carmen.
As the hills became more popular, the number of activities offered to tourists also increased. One of them is ATV ride that goes through the foothills. It would be unwise not to try it. So we did. We rode through the terrain for about an hour with stops on selected spots for a couple of pictures. Each group is assigned with a tour guide and included on his services is being a photographer. These guys have templates on what pose is good for which background. And you are obliged to do so. :D
It was fun but I would advise to do it early in the morning or late in the afternoon unless you want to suffer from 32 degrees under the sun. ;)
